LAST UPDATED: OCTOBER 2025

+ QUICK SUMMARY: SIMPLY TOOLS PROCESSES ALL DATA LOCALLY IN YOUR BROWSER. WE DON'T COLLECT, STORE, OR TRANSMIT YOUR PERSONAL FILES OR DATA TO ANY SERVERS. YOUR PRIVACY IS OUR PRIORITY.
|--| DATA CONTROLLER INFORMATION

THIS PRIVACY POLICY APPLIES TO SIMPLY TOOLS, A COLLECTION OF WEB-BASED UTILITIES HOSTED AT [YOUR-DOMAIN.COM]. FOR ANY PRIVACY-RELATED INQUIRIES, PLEASE CONTACT US USING THE INFORMATION PROVIDED AT THE END OF THIS POLICY.

|--| WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

SIMPLY TOOLS IS DESIGNED WITH PRIVACY IN MIND. HERE'S WHAT WE DO AND DON'T COLLECT:

+ WE DO NOT COLLECT:
- YOUR UPLOADED FILES OR DOCUMENTS
- PERSONAL INFORMATION ENTERED INTO OUR TOOLS
- PROCESSED DATA OR CONVERSION RESULTS
- ACCOUNT INFORMATION (NO REGISTRATION REQUIRED)

WE MAY COLLECT LIMITED TECHNICAL DATA:

  • BASIC WEBSITE ANALYTICS (PAGE VIEWS, GENERAL LOCATION)
  • ERROR LOGS FOR DEBUGGING PURPOSES
  • BROWSER TYPE AND VERSION FOR COMPATIBILITY
  • IP ADDRESS (ANONYMIZED IN ANALYTICS)
|--| HOW OUR TOOLS WORK

ALL SIMPLY TOOLS OPERATE ENTIRELY WITHIN YOUR WEB BROWSER USING CLIENT-SIDE PROCESSING:

  • LOCAL PROCESSING: FILES ARE PROCESSED DIRECTLY IN YOUR BROWSER USING JAVASCRIPT
  • NO SERVER UPLOAD: YOUR FILES NEVER LEAVE YOUR DEVICE OR GET UPLOADED TO OUR SERVERS
  • TEMPORARY STORAGE: DATA IS ONLY STORED TEMPORARILY IN BROWSER MEMORY DURING PROCESSING
  • AUTOMATIC CLEANUP: ALL DATA IS CLEARED WHEN YOU CLOSE THE TAB OR NAVIGATE AWAY
|--| COOKIES AND LOCAL STORAGE

WE USE MINIMAL COOKIES AND LOCAL STORAGE FOR ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONALITY:

  • FUNCTIONAL COOKIES: REMEMBER YOUR PREFERENCES (THEME, SETTINGS)
  • ANALYTICS COOKIES: BASIC USAGE STATISTICS (CAN BE DISABLED)
  • NO TRACKING: WE DON'T USE COOKIES FOR ADVERTISING OR CROSS-SITE TRACKING

YOU CAN DISABLE COOKIES IN YOUR BROWSER SETTINGS WITHOUT AFFECTING CORE TOOL FUNCTIONALITY.

|--| ANALYTICS AND THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

WE MAY USE PRIVACY-FOCUSED ANALYTICS SERVICES TO UNDERSTAND HOW OUR TOOLS ARE USED:

  • GOOGLE ANALYTICS (ANONYMIZED IP, NO PERSONAL DATA)
  • CONTENT DELIVERY NETWORKS (CDNS) FOR LOADING EXTERNAL LIBRARIES
  • KO-FI FOR DONATION PROCESSING (ONLY IF YOU CHOOSE TO DONATE)

THESE SERVICES HAVE THEIR OWN PRIVACY POLICIES. WE CHOOSE PROVIDERS THAT RESPECT USER PRIVACY AND ALLOW YOU TO OPT OUT.

|--| DATA SECURITY

SINCE WE DON'T STORE YOUR FILES OR PERSONAL DATA ON OUR SERVERS, THE SECURITY RISKS ARE MINIMAL:

  • HTTPS ENCRYPTION: ALL COMMUNICATIONS ARE ENCRYPTED IN TRANSIT
  • NO DATA RETENTION: NO FILES OR PROCESSED DATA ARE STORED ON OUR SERVERS
  • BROWSER SECURITY: PROCESSING HAPPENS IN YOUR SECURE BROWSER ENVIRONMENT
  • REGULAR UPDATES: WE KEEP OUR CODE AND DEPENDENCIES UPDATED FOR SECURITY

THE MINIMAL ANALYTICS DATA WE COLLECT MAY BE PROCESSED BY THIRD-PARTY SERVICES LOCATED IN DIFFERENT COUNTRIES, BUT THIS DATA IS ANONYMIZED AND CONTAINS NO PERSONAL INFORMATION.
